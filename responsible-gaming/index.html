Responsible Gaming on Agni Club

Online entertainment should always remain safe, controlled and enjoyable. This guide is built around a simple idea: treat the activity as entertainment, never as a source of income.

Responsible-Play Guidelines

  1. Set a budget before you play. Decide a spending cap and never exceed it.
  2. Manage your time. Avoid long unbroken sessions — short breaks help.
  3. Play for entertainment only. Don't treat it as fixed income.
  4. Do not chase losses. Increasing bets to recover losses raises risk fast.
  5. Stay emotionally balanced. Skip sessions when angry, tired or stressed.
  6. Keep your account secure. Never share passwords or OTPs.
  7. Follow age restrictions. 18+ only.

Signs You Should Take a Break

  • You're spending more than your planned budget.
  • You're playing for too many hours each day.
  • You're hiding play time from family or friends.
  • You're borrowing money to keep playing.
  • You're using play as a way to escape stress.

Safer Play Tips

  • Use the in-app limit settings if available.
  • Take a 24-hour cool-off after any large loss.
  • Talk to someone you trust about your play habits.

Get Help

If you or someone you know struggles with online play, please reach out to a licensed counsellor or a local helpline. Support is always confidential.
