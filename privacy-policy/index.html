Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how the Agni Club informational blog handles visitor information.

Information We Collect

This website is informational. We collect only anonymous analytics (page views, referrers) to understand which articles are useful. We do not collect names, email addresses or financial information unless you voluntarily contact us.

Cookies

We may use first-party cookies for basic analytics and preference storage. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time without losing access to content.

Third-Party Services

Embedded fonts (Google Fonts) and analytics services may set their own cookies according to their published policies.

Your Rights

You can request information about, correction of, or deletion of any personal data you have voluntarily shared with us by emailing editor@agniclub.info.

Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. The latest version always lives at this URL.
