Agni Club Guide

Agni Club Register

Creating an Agni Club account is a quick, beginner-friendly process. This guide covers what you need, how the flow works, and how to keep your new account secure from day one.

What You Need Before You Start

  • An active mobile number that can receive SMS.
  • A strong password (8+ characters, mixed case, numbers).
  • An optional invite code shared by an existing member.

Registration Steps

  1. Open the official Agni Club app or website.
  2. Tap Register.
  3. Enter your mobile number and tap Get OTP.
  4. Type the OTP you receive over SMS.
  5. Set a strong password and confirm it.
  6. (Optional) Enter an invite code.
  7. Accept the terms and tap Create Account.

Try the Register Flow (Preview Only)

This is a preview form. Use the official Agni Club app to register your real account.

Tips for a Secure New Account

  • Use a unique password — never reuse passwords from email or banking apps.
  • Don't share your OTP with anyone, ever, under any circumstance.
  • Bind your bank account only when you're ready to deposit or withdraw, not before.

After Registration

You can now explore the in-app sections, claim any first-time bonus that may be active, and review the responsible-play settings before you play your first round.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to enter an invite code?

No — invite codes are optional. They simply track who referred you for the bonus program.

Can I register without a mobile number?

No — OTP verification on a mobile number is required.

Is there a registration fee?

No, registration itself is free.
