A Safe Play Checklist for Agni Club Users

Eight habits that separate casual, enjoyable play from problem behaviour — written for anyone who wants to keep entertainment as entertainment.

Why a Checklist Helps

A short, written checklist removes decision-making in the moment. Habits replace impulses. Here are eight that consistently keep play in the entertainment column.

The Eight Habits

  1. Set a weekly entertainment budget — never exceed it.
  2. Pre-decide your session length.
  3. Never play to recover a previous loss.
  4. Take a short break between sessions.
  5. Avoid play under stress, fatigue or alcohol.
  6. Don't borrow money to play.
  7. Talk openly with someone you trust about your habits.
  8. If you can't stop, stop. Use the in-app limit tools or seek professional help.

Final Word

Apps are designed to be entertaining, and they succeed at that. Keeping the entertainment from becoming something else is your responsibility — and it's a habit, not a one-off decision.
