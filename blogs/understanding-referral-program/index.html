Understanding the Agni Club Referral Program

The {BRAND} referral system has tiers, conditions and timing rules. This article unpacks how each piece works in plain English.

How Referrals Work

You share your invite code; someone signs up using it; you get credit when they hit certain activity milestones. The simplicity ends there — the interesting part is the tier structure and the timing.

Tier Structure

The referral system pays more as the invited member's first-deposit size and betting volume grow. The exact numbers vary, but the principle is consistent: bigger and more active referrals unlock bigger rewards.

Timing Rules

Rewards usually post after the invited member meets a minimum activity window (a multiplier of their first deposit). This avoids gaming the system with rapid sign-ups.

What Counts as Fair Play

  • One person, one account.
  • No fake numbers, no shared payment instruments.
  • No artificially boosted bets just to unlock referral bonuses.
