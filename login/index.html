Agni Club Login

Signing in to Agni Club is fast and beginner-friendly. This guide explains the official login screen, the inputs required, and how to recover access if something goes wrong.

Before You Log In

Make sure you already have an account. If you don't, head to the Register guide first. Keep your registered mobile number and password ready.

Steps to Log In

  1. Open the official Agni club app or web page.
  2. Tap the Log In option on the home screen.
  3. Enter your registered mobile number.
  4. Enter your password.
  5. Tap Sign In to access your dashboard.

Try the Login Flow (Preview Only)

This is a preview form. Use the official Agni Club app to log in to your real account.

Forgot Password?

Tap the Forgot Password link on the login screen. You'll receive an OTP on your registered number. Set a new strong password and sign in again.

Common Sign-In Issues

  • OTP not received: Check signal, switch networks, then retry after a minute.
  • Wrong password: Use the reset flow rather than guessing repeatedly.
  • App version outdated: Download the latest version from the official source.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I log in to Agni Club on multiple devices?

One active session per account is the safest practice. Avoid sharing credentials.

Is my password visible to support?

No — your password should never be requested by support staff.

Why was I logged out automatically?

Most often a routine session refresh. Sign in again and you're good.
